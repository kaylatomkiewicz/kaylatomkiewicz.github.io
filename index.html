My name is Kayla Tomkiewicz

I'm a designer and I invite you to look around. If you like what you see, shoot me an e-mail. If you don't like what you see, give me a shout anyways and we can talk about it.

I have known so many good people. Wonderful, creative, and passionate people who wanted to share their ideas and values with the world and be heard

Whether it was in running a restaurant, an equine ranch, or simply looking for a full-time career, these people all had one thing in common; they couldn’t successfully thrive in their situations. While they had the qualifications, ambition, and the market that was required to succeed in their field, they failed in making their passions known to their audiences. They couldn’t properly communicate and market their business/skills on a larger scale.

The ability to provide a solid brand identity and an effective marketing solution is instrumental in providing people with the business and livelihood they deserve. Print is a powerful thing. If you can gain the viewer’s attention within 2 seconds, you’re already halfway there. I want to make sure that people are represented to their fullest potential. That is why I chose to be a designer. And I love what I do.
